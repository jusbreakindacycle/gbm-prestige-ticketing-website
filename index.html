What is the 8-in-1 Ticketing System?

Eight assisted service categories, explained simply.

The “8-in-1 Ticketing System” is a marketing slogan used to describe eight service categories that the business can assist customers with. It does not mean a fully automated booking platform.

Customers are encouraged to contact the business directly for inquiry, confirmation, and next steps.
